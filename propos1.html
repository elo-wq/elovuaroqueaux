éloïse vuaroqueaux







Eloïse Vuaroqueaux is a Paris-based artist working with photography. After graduating from the Sorbonne with a degree in Geography, she entered the Photography Master's program at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure Louis-Lumière in 2022. Her photographic work is based on a hybrid exploration. She develops an experimental universe in which she manipulates digital material to create new worlds. Interested in the concept of healing, she creates metal sculptures in which she integrates 3D materials evoking bodies. Through this approach, she seeks to understand how the use of scientific imagery in contemporary art feeds our imagination.
